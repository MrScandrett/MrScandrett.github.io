STEAM Time-Traveler Library

Classic science fiction organized by the big idea each book planted in human imagination — long before the technology existed to make it real.

Verne imagined submarines — submarines were built. Wells imagined time travel — physicists started talking about spacetime. Science often begins as a story someone was brave enough to imagine. These books are not "English class stuff." They are the imagination engine that preceded real technology. Every one of them is free, legal to own, and waiting for you.

Student Recipe Template

Use this structure when you submit a build idea so every project reads clearly for the next student.

Required parts

  • Project title
  • Pathway and difficulty
  • Estimated time
  • Materials and tools
  • What you make
  • Step-by-step directions

Quality check

  • One sentence hook at the top
  • Short steps, not giant paragraphs
  • Common mistakes to avoid
  • One extension or challenge mode
  • Photo, render, or example if possible
  • Clear enough that another student could follow it

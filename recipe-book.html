Student Recipe Template

Use this structure when you submit a build idea so every project reads clearly for the next student.

Required parts

  • Project title
  • Pathway and difficulty
  • Estimated time
  • Materials and tools
  • What you make
  • Step-by-step directions

Quality check

  • One sentence hook at the top
  • Short steps, not giant paragraphs
  • Common mistakes to avoid
  • One extension or challenge mode
  • Photo, render, or example if possible
  • Clear enough that another student could follow it

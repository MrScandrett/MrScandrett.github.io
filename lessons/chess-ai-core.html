AI Timeline · Lesson 05

AI Chess Through History

One lesson, multiple historical opponents: compare how chess AI evolved from early heuristic play to modern engines.

Play Against Historical AI Models

Play as White on a full 8x8 board, then switch AI eras to see how search depth and heuristics change behavior.

How This Timeline Version Works

  • BoardStandard 8x8 setup with all chess pieces.
  • GoalWin by checkmate (or draw by stalemate).
  • ModelsEach model era changes search depth, style, and consistency.
  • ScopeNo castling or en passant in this classroom build.

    Turn In (End of Class)

    Learning Goals

    • Goal 1Explain how chess AI strategy changed from 1950s heuristics to modern engines
    • Goal 2Compare model behavior under different search depths
    • Goal 3Connect evaluation heuristics to move quality and consistency
    • Goal 4Use game evidence to argue which model style is strongest and why

    Challenge Tasks

    • Task 1Beat two different model eras in one class period
    • Task 2Record one move where two models choose different plans
    • Task 3Increase depth and explain one visible behavior change
    • Task 4Propose one heuristic tweak to improve the weakest model

    Lesson Outputs

    Each team should publish a short comparison reflection at the end of class.

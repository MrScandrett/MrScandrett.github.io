About This Model

🌿 Carbon Cycle

CO₂ and CH₄ trap outgoing infrared radiation. The model uses the IPCC radiative forcing formula: ΔF = 5.35 × ln(C/C₀) for CO₂, scaled by the methane forcing contribution. Forests remove ~2.6 Gt CO₂/yr — deforestation reduces this sink.

☀️ Energy Balance

Earth's temperature is set by the balance of incoming solar energy and outgoing thermal radiation. Ice and snow reflect sunlight (high albedo). As ice melts, the darker ocean absorbs more heat — a positive feedback loop that amplifies warming.

🌊 Ocean Systems

Oceans absorb ~90% of excess heat and ~30% of CO₂ emissions, slowing surface warming but causing acidification. As pH drops below 8.0, coral reefs and shellfish are threatened. Warmer seas fuel more intense hurricanes and alter global currents.

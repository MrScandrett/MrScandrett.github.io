Perceptron Lab

Train a one-layer classifier from weighted inputs and visualize why linear decision boundaries matter.

Learning Goals

  • Goal 1Describe weighted sums and activation thresholds
  • Goal 2Compare correct and incorrect classifications
  • Goal 3Tune weights to reduce obvious errors

  • Task 2Implement prediction from weights and bias
  • Task 3Add a simple update rule for training
  • Task 4Chart results before and after training

History Panel: Mark I Perceptron (1958)

The specific machine demonstrated in 1958 was the Mark I Perceptron.

Machine Context

  • ScientistFrank Rosenblatt
  • InstitutionCornell
  • Hardware ContextImplemented on an IBM 704 computer
  • Physical MachineMark I Perceptron

Use this distinction in class: Perceptron is the general model; Mark I Perceptron was the demonstrated physical machine.

AI ORIGINS · LESSON 03

MARK I PERCEPTRON DEMO CONSOLE

START: Make the line guess right.

This machine tries to separate dots with ONE straight line. Training moves the line when a guess is wrong. AND and OR can fit one line. XOR cannot.

What to do

  1. Click AND: WORKING CASE
  2. Click RESET WEIGHTS
  3. Click TRAIN BURST (5)

What to watch

  • The line turns and slides.
  • Red glow = wrong guesses.

When red glow shrinks, learning is happening.

Try these

AND (works)

Train a few times.

Goal: get most dots right with one line.

OR (works)

Click OR. Reset. Train.

Goal: it learns a new rule with the same machine.

XOR (can’t with one line)

Click XOR.

Try LINE ANGLE + LINE OFFSET.

Goal: notice you can’t make it perfect. That’s the lesson.

Training moves the line when it is wrong.

Show the math (older students)

z = (w1*x1 + w2*x2) + b
y^ = step(z)

Short rule:

w = w + eta * error * x

Why XOR fails here:

One layer draws one straight boundary. Hidden layers can curve the boundary.

Limit Notes

WHAT THIS IS

A perceptron is a small guess machine. It learns by moving one straight line.

WHAT IT CAN'T DO

XOR cannot be solved by one line in two dimensions. This is a structural limit, not just undertraining.

HOW TO USE IT LIKE IT'S 1958

  • Set channels to (1,0). Run.
  • Try 0.1 learning rate first. Watch what changes.
  • Adjust W1 upward until output flips.
  • Switch to XOR. Can you draw one line that separates all points? Try.

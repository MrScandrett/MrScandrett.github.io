Mars Rover Landing

You're entering Martian atmosphere. Fight gravity and wind, manage your fuel, and land precisely on the pad. Too fast or off-target — and it's over.

🔴 Mars Gravity

3.72 m/s² — about 38% of Earth's. Objects fall slower, but you still need thrust to slow your descent.

💨 Martian Wind

Mars has thin but persistent winds. A new wind direction and strength is set each game — watch the indicator.

⛽ Fuel Management

Every thruster burn costs fuel. If you run out before landing, you'll fall uncontrolled. Burn smart.

🎯 Landing Criteria

Hit the pad with vertical speed < 4 m/s, horizontal speed < 2.5 m/s, and tilt within 12°.

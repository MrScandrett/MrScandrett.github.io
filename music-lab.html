Create + Sound

Music Lab

Plug in a USB MIDI keyboard, then press Enable Audio. Choose a preset and play. Drums map across common GM drum notes and the pads below.

Connection

Audio: off · MIDI: off

Notes
  • WebMIDI usually requires HTTPS and a supported browser (Chrome/Edge).
  • Piano is a lightweight synthesized approximation for fast classroom load times.
  • Drums trigger on note-on. Pads below are clickable and touch-friendly.

Piano Roll + Drum Pads

Press and drag your mouse (or finger) across the keys to play notes.

Tip: Set preset to Drums for MIDI-triggered drums.

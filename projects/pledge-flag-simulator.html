USA Civics / Ritual

American Flag Waving Simulator

Stand, read, and recite together. Optional MIDI patriotic backing track.

Pledge of Allegiance

I pledge allegiance to the Flag of the United States of America, and to the Republic for which it stands, one Nation under God, indivisible, with liberty and justice for all.

Note: browsers don’t natively play .mid files reliably. This demo uses an .mp3 placeholder by default. If you want true MIDI playback, load a tiny MIDI synth library or pre-render to audio.

How to Use

  • Press Play, stand, and read together.
  • Optional: students take turns leading the pledge.
  • Engineering extension: tweak wave speed + amplitude in CSS.

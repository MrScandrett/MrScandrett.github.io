USA Civics / Ritual

American Flag Waving Simulator

Stand, read, and recite together with a 50-star flag drawn to federal-style proportions and an optional classroom backing track.

Flag geometry now follows the 50-star proportions used in Executive Order 10834.

Pledge of Allegiance

I pledge allegiance to the Flag of the United States of America, and to the Republic for which it stands, one Nation under God, indivisible, with liberty and justice for all.

Respect Mode: stand if able, face the flag, and place your right hand over your heart. Veterans and active-duty service members may salute.

Show etiquette
  • Stand if you are able.
  • Face the flag and place your right hand over your heart.
  • Remove hats unless worn for religious reasons.
  • Uniformed active-duty and veterans may salute.
  • This simulator is for civic learning and respectful classroom practice.

Note: this page now ships with a small built-in backing track so playback works on GitHub Pages. Swap the audio file if you want a different recording for class.

Backing track ready.

How to Use

  • Press Play, stand, and read together.
  • Use Fullscreen for whole-class recitation.
  • Set Wind to calm, medium, or strong based on classroom tone.
  • Optional: students take turns leading the pledge.
  • Engineering extension: tune the SVG displacement filter to compare calm and stormy motion.
